Our story

Your Neighborhood Handyman Since 2008

To provide Irving homeowners with reliable, high-quality handyman services that simplify home maintenance and improve living spaces.

Tony's Remodeling started as a small operation with a single truck and a passion for craftsmanship. Founder Tony Martinez saw a need for honest, skilled handyman services in Irving that homeowners could trust. Over the years, word spread about our attention to detail and fair pricing, and we grew into a full-service team serving the entire Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex.

Today, we remain committed to the same values: integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction. Every project, big or small, receives the same level of care and professionalism. We're proud to be a part of the Irving community and look forward to helping you love your home.
