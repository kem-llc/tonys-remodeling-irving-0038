Our story

Your Local Handyman with a Commitment to Excellence

At Tony's Remodeling, our mission is to provide Irving homeowners and businesses with reliable, high-quality handyman services that make life easier. We believe in honesty, transparency, and craftsmanship.

Tony's Remodeling started with a simple idea: give Irving a handyman service that actually cares. With over 20 years of experience in construction and home improvement, our founder Tony saw a need for a company that combines skilled trades with old-fashioned customer service. We built our reputation one job at a time, earning trust through reliability and quality.

Today, we're proud to be a go-to resource for the Irving community. Our team of licensed professionals brings expertise in plumbing, electrical, carpentry, and remodeling. We treat every home like our own and every client like a neighbor. Whether it's a quick fix or a major renovation, we're here to help.
